about Xing Tong

Tianjin Xingtong Petroleum Equipment Co., Ltd. was established in 2012, is a professional manufacturer that is concerned with the design, development and production of spare parts for submersible pump motor and pump assembly. It covers an area of 25,000 square meters, and have over 450 employees, belonging to national high-tech enterprise, it has passed ISO9001 quality management system certification£¬ has awarded multiple national patents and honors such as, provincial enterprise technology research and development center, famous trademark of Tianjin, Tianjin patent demonstration enterprise, clean production enterprise. All of our products comply with international quality standards and are greatly appreciated in a variety of different markets throughout the world, including domestic Shengli, Daqing, Liaohe, Dagang oil fields and exported to international markets such as USA, Russia, Indonesia, Singapore, and so on.
